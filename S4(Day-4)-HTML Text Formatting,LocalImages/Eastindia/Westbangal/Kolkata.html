Kolkata is a 3rd metropolitan city with a population of more than 14 million.

Description:-

Kolkata, formerly known as Calcutta, is the capital of West Bengal in eastern India and one of the country's major cultural and educational hubs. Known as the "City of Joy," it’s renowned for its vibrant festivals, historical landmarks, and literary heritage. Kolkata is famous for its colonial architecture, especially along the iconic Hooghly River, which flows through the city. Notable landmarks include the grand Victoria Memorial, Howrah Bridge, and Dakshineswar Kali Temple. The city is also known for its love of arts, literature, and theater, as well as its delicious Bengali cuisine, which features dishes like fish curry, sweets like rosogolla, and street foods like puchka. Kolkata's cultural scene is vibrant, with events like Durga Puja celebrated grandly. It is also home to one of India’s oldest and largest book fairs and has a reputation for producing some of the country's finest poets, artists, and intellectuals.
