Mumbai is a 1st metropolitan city with a population of more than 18 million.

Description:-

Mumbai, often called the "City of Dreams," is the bustling financial and entertainment capital of India. Located on the western coast, it is known for its stunning Marine Drive and the iconic Gateway of India. A city of contrasts, Mumbai houses India's largest slums alongside luxurious skyscrapers and elite neighborhoods. The city is a melting pot of diverse cultures, languages, and cuisines, reflecting India’s rich heritage. Known for its lively nightlife, historic Bollywood film industry, and street food like vada pav and pav bhaji, Mumbai is vibrant and always buzzing. With a humid tropical climate and one of the busiest ports in the country, Mumbai is also an economic powerhouse and a center for trade, technology, and innovation. The famous local trains and dabbawalas (lunchbox deliverymen) showcase the city’s spirit of resilience and teamwork.
